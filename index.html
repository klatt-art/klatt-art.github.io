About

Sara Barbara Klatt, born in 1999, is a dedicated artist from Poland whose creative adventure began in early childhood. Immersed in art from the age of six and actively exploring painting, drawing, and sculpture by thirteen, she has developed a multifaceted artistic practice.

An early fascination with baroque art and human anatomy set the stage for her diverse work. Sara refined her skills at a music school, enriched her perspective at an art high school in Olsztyn, and earned her master’s degree at the Academy of Fine Arts in Katowice, specializing in painting with an added focus on sculpture.

She experiments with various techniques primarily employing oil paints and oil sprays to explore a broad range of themes. Her work spans from evocative floral expressions that reveal inner beauty to explorations of facial expressions, the raw allure of naked human anatomy, and dramatic contrasts between light and dark. Additionally, she has ventured into the delicate balance between restraint and liberation.

Each creation stands as a testament to her ever-evolving artistic adventure.
